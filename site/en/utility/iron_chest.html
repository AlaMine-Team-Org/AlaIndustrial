Iron Chest

alaindustrial:iron_chest
UtilityImplementedstoragechestutility

Summary

The Iron Chest is pure item storage with 36 slots (4 rows × 9) — one row more than the vanilla 27-slot chest. It isn't tied to the mod's energy network: it's a "civilian" storage block, an upgrade over the vanilla copper chest (minecraft:copper_chest from the Copper Age of 26.2). It's crafted with a copper chest in the centre, surrounded by 8 iron ingots. It renders as a real 3D chest model with an animated lid (like the vanilla wooden chest) and its own iron.png texture (64×64, same UV layout). Opening/closing plays a custom iron_chest_open/iron_chest_close sound. Unlike the mod's machines, its block entity isn't built on MachineBlockEntity (which requires energy) but on the vanilla base — this is the mod's first content block with no energy dependency.

Interface

Type: container (alaindustrial:iron_chest). GUI atlas: assets/alaindustrial/textures/gui/container/iron_chest.png (256×256, the visible part 176×184 — taller than the standard 166 because of the 4th row). No energy/progress indicators (the chest isn't a machine). Both labels sit on the left: the "Iron Chest" title above the chest grid (titleLabelX = 8) and the "Inventory" label in the gap between the chest and the player inventory (inventoryLabelX = 8, inventoryLabelY = 92).

Rendering and animation

3D model (on 26.2): reuses the vanilla ChestModel (lid/lock/bottom, createSingleBodyLayer), baked from its own alaindustrial:iron_chest model. Texture — assets/alaindustrial/textures/entity/chest/iron.png (64×64, the format of vanilla entity/chest/normal). Supplied via the atlas-sprite path — like the vanilla renderer, which gives correct depth/cull/mipmaps (no white outline). Lid animation via ChestLidController: openness is interpolated by the client ticker; the 1 - (1-open)³ curve matches vanilla. Facing transform — a yaw around the top-centre. Item icon — a 3D special renderer (items/iron_chest.jsontype: minecraft:special, model: {type: minecraft:chest, texture: alaindustrial:iron} + base: minecraft:item/chest). Marketing 3D render — tools/iron_chest_3d.png (128px, upscaled to 512 in the asset pack; a closed chest with lid, isometric, transparent background). Generated procedurally from the model texture entity/chest/iron.png along vanilla chest geometry (ChestModel.createSingleBodyLayer — bottom 14×10×14 + lid 14×5×14 + lock 2×4×1) by the tools/gen_chest_3d.py script; the cubic render_cube doesn't apply here — vanilla chest geometry can't be expressed as flat cube faces.

Sound

iron_chest_open / iron_chest_close — generated via ElevenLabs SFX (the same candidate texture close_wood_02 used for both), converted MP3→OGG (mono, 44.1kHz, Vorbis). Played from .onOpen/onClose via playSound(...): source SoundSource.BLOCKS, volume 0.42 (quieter than vanilla's 0.5 — the chest sound is bright), random pitch 0.9–1.0. Subtitles: subtitles.alaindustrial.iron_chest_open / ..._close (full localization, 20 languages).

Recipes

Crafting: data/alaindustrial/recipe/iron_chest.json Type: minecraft:crafting_shaped, category redstone. Pattern 3×3: III / ICI / III. C = #minecraft:copper_chests (any oxidation stage + waxed), I = minecraft:iron_ingot. Result: alaindustrial:iron_chest.

Compatibility

Recipe viewers: JEI, REI, EMI (via the existing AlaReiPlugin). Hoppers: the chest implements the vanilla container interface, so hoppers/pipes can insert/extract items the standard vanilla way (symmetric IO — no slot roles, since all slots are the same).

Progression

Chest line: vanilla copper_chest (27) → Iron Chest (36) → (potentially) higher tiers (silver/gold/diamond — not documented).

Related

Battery Box — the mod's other "storage" block, but for energy. Vanilla minecraft:copper_chest — the predecessor in the crafting recipe.
