Tin Ore

alaindustrial:tin_ore
MaterialsImplementedmaterialmetalsoresworldgen

Summary

Tin is the mod's earliest metal: the base of the wires and circuits that LV machines run on. Its ore spawns in stone and in deepslate, and is mined with any stone pickaxe or better. There are two routes out of one vein. A furnace yields 1 ingot with no machines. Run the block of ore through a macerator, though, and it breaks into 2 dust — each of which smelts into an ingot, for 2 ingots per block, twice the yield. There is no vanilla tin, so the ingot is a mod item. The tin form family (all implemented):

Recipes

The main processing path runs through the raw form: ore → raw → ingot. Smelting the ore directly into an ingot is kept as a fallback route that needs no macerator. The macerator only doubles the yield of a whole ore block (2 dust); the raw form and a finished ingot grind down to 1 dust with no doubling. Furnace/electric furnace, raw → ingot (main path): minecraft:smelting/blasting, recipe/tin_ingot_from_smelting_raw_tin.json + a _blasting_ variant; the electric furnace uses recipe/smelting/raw_tin.json (energy: 200) — analogous to raw_copper/raw_iron. Furnace/electric furnace, ore → ingot (kept, parity with vanilla copper): recipe/tin_ingot_from_smelting_tin_ore.json + deepslate/_blasting_ variants, no doubling. Maceration of the ore block → dust ×2: recipe/maceration/tin.json (result{count:2}, energy: 300). Maceration of raw_tin → dust ×1: recipe/maceration/raw_tin.json. Smelting dust → ingot 1:1: recipe/smelting/tin_dust.json (energy: 200, electric furnace). Maceration of the ingot → dust ×1 (re-processing): recipe/maceration/tin_ingot.json.

Progression

Tier: none (material). Leads into: the Electronic Circuit — tin and copper wire are assembled into it, and every LV machine needs at least one. It is the early-crafting bottleneck. Requires: a working Macerator and a furnace/electric furnace (smelting).

Compatibility

Recipe viewers: JEI, REI, EMI (ore, raw, dust, ingot as ingredients/results) — once the recipes exist.
