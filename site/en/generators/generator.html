LV Generator

alaindustrial:generator
GeneratorsLVImplementedtier-lvenergyfuel

Summary

The Generator is the mod's first source of power. It burns solid fuel (coal, charcoal, blocks of coal and the like) and produces EU/FE into an internal buffer, releasing it to neighbouring receivers. This is the entry point into LV-tier energy, feeding the early machines.

Throughput: not applicable on the input side (the generator does not accept external EU/FE). The output is LV (up to 32 EU/FE per tick), so feeding LV cables and LV machines downstream is safe. Faces: EU/FE is emitted from 5 faces (all except the front, FACING); the front face is inert — cables don't connect to it and hoppers don't work through it.

Balance

Base: 8 EU/FE/tick, 4000 EU/FE buffer. Rationale: at 8 EU/FE/tick a single generator steadily powers one LV machine with a moderate draw (see the Macerator — 2 EU/FE/tick) and still leaves headroom to recharge the buffer. The 4000 EU/FE buffer (≈6.7 s at full output) smooths the pause when swapping fuel, but it doesn't turn the generator into a storage block — that's the role of the Battery Box. These numbers are deliberate starter values for balance tuning, not copied from other mods.
