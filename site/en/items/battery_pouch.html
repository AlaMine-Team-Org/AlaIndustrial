Battery Pouch

alaindustrial:battery_pouch
ItemsLVImplementeditemstorageenergypouch

Summary

The Battery Pouch is a portable EU/FE-powered item-storage — the mod's first electric item and the foundation of the "item energy core" (ItemEnergy) for future electric tools and armour. It works like the vanilla bundle but holds twice as much (128 weight ≈ 2 stacks) and requires a charge: while items sit inside, the pouch passively drains 1 EU/FE/sec; at 0 EU/FE the "lock" closes — you can neither put items in nor take them out until the pouch is recharged in a Battery Box slot. Charge, contents and name (anvil) are stored in data components and survive drop/death/transfer.

Recipes

Crafting: data/alaindustrial/recipe/battery_pouch.json — shaped 3×3 (a leather body, a tin clasp, a vanilla bundle in the centre, an Electronic Circuit at the bottom):

L T L L = minecraft:leather
L B L T = alaindustrial:tin_ingot
L C L B = minecraft:bundle
C = alaindustrial:electronic_circuit

Plus a recipe_unlocked advancement (the recipe appears in the recipe book once all ingredients — leather, tin, a circuit, a bundle — have been obtained).

Behavior

Insertion: right-click a stack onto the pouch in an inventory (overrideOtherStackedOnMe), or the pouch onto a stack in a slot (overrideStackedOnOther). Requires EU/FE > 0. Auto-merge: first tops up an existing partial stack of the same item, then takes up new weight. Any leftover that doesn't fit by weight stays in hand. Sound minecraft:item.bundle.insert. Extraction: right-click the pouch in the air (use) or right-click the pouch on an empty slot — pulls the whole top stack (LIFO by stack; decision Q-EXT-1). Requires EU/FE > 0 and non-empty contents. Sound minecraft:item.bundle.remove_one. Lock: at 0 EU/FE both operations are rejected with minecraft:item.bundle.insert_fail. Contents are NOT lost — locked until recharged. Passive drain: inventoryTick (server-only in 26.2, all player inventory slots) — every 20 ticks −lvPouchDrainPerSecond EU/FE, only if items are inside and EU/FE > 0. It does NOT drain in a chest / on the ground / in a Battery Box slot (inventoryTick isn't called there). Indication: the item bar under the icon = EU/FE charge, colour EnergyTier.LV.color (0xFFD24A), always visible (the bar position is the vanilla render, x+2 / width 13). Tooltip: a two-item usage list, the charge (a warning at <10%, a red "discharged" at 0); plus a bundle-style visual block (PouchTooltip/PouchClientTooltip): a grid of ALL stored stacks (the top one first; 4 columns, 8 with 9+ stacks) and a fill bar with "weight / 128" numbers. The charge component is built with ignoreSwapAnimation — the per-second drain doesn't twitch the in-hand item animation. The LV tier line is removed — the pouch is positioned as a tier-neutral consumer item; the charge and item bar are enough. Restrictions: a pouch can't be nested into a pouch; nesting into a vanilla bundle/shulker is allowed (weight 64 / 1 slot respectively). Rename: the vanilla anvil (CUSTOM_NAME) — no special code. Drop/death: the components are persistent — charge and contents are kept on the item.

Progression

The first electric item; the future line: MV (256 weight, filters), HV (512, linking) — see the design doc §9. Available after the Battery Box (without it the pouch can't be charged).

Compatibility

Recipe viewers: JEI, REI, EMI (as a regular item). Charging by other mods (TR Energy / NeoForge energy) — out of MVP scope; bridges will be added once ≥2 electric items exist.
