Tempered Iron

alaindustrial:tempered_iron
ComponentsImplementedcomponentmetalstempered-iron

Summary

Tempered Iron is an ingot obtained by smelting the vanilla minecraft:iron_ingot. It's the source material for the mod's entire same-named line: five tools, a four-piece armour set, and a 9:1 material block. It's an item, not a block — equipment and block recipes reference it as an ingredient. Its role is close to the vanilla iron_ingot, but it's a separate material — a smelted (tempered) ingot that's slightly better than iron in the items made from it.

Recipes

Three ways to obtain the ingot from minecraft:iron_ingot (all 1:1): Vanilla furnace (recipe/tempered_iron_from_smelting_iron_ingot.json, minecraft:smelting): 1× iron_ingot → 1× tempered_iron; XP 0.5, 200 ticks. Blast furnace (recipe/tempered_iron_from_blasting_iron_ingot.json, minecraft:blasting): 1× iron_ingot → 1× tempered_iron; XP 0.5, 100 ticks (twice as fast as the furnace, as always for the blast furnace). Mod electric furnace (recipe/smelting/iron_ingot.json, alaindustrial:smelting): 1× iron_ingot → 1× tempered_iron; energy: 200 EU/FE (at 2 EU/FE/t → 100 ticks). No XP (XP is a vanilla furnace mechanic; the mod's electric furnace doesn't grant it). The reverse route — disassembling the material block (1 block → 9 ingots, shapeless) — is described in the Tempered Iron Block spec.

Progression

Tier: none (component/material). Used in: Tools (pickaxe/axe/hoe/shovel/sword) — crafted by the vanilla shaped recipes of the iron equivalents, 1–3 ingots per item. Armour (helmet/chestplate/leggings/boots) — shaped recipes of the iron equivalents, 4–8 ingots per item. Tempered Iron Block (9:1) — for compact storage and building. Repair: tools and armour are repaired in an anvil with ingots via the tags #alaindustrial:tempered_iron_tool_materials and #alaindustrial:tempered_iron_armor_materials (each contains exactly alaindustrial:tempered_iron) — analogous to the vanilla #minecraft:iron_tool_materials / #minecraft:repairs_iron_armor.

Compatibility

Recipe viewers: JEI, REI, EMI — all three smelting recipes are visible as ways to obtain the ingot.

Related

Tempered Iron Block — the compact 9:1 form. Tempered Iron tools — the main consumer of the ingots. Tempered Iron Pickaxe — a separate spec for the first tool. Material Forms — dust/plate/billet of the mod's ores (a related metallurgy chain).
