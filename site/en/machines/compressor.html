Compressor

alaindustrial:compressor
MachinesLVImplementedtier-lvprocessingmetals

Summary

The Compressor is an LV machine that squeezes raw materials into denser forms: metal dust into ingots, coal and gem dust back into items, ice and snow into denser blocks, sugar cane into paper. Its standout job is closing the ore-doubling loop for non-metals — it's the only machine that restores coal, diamond, emerald and lapis dust to a usable item, so ore → 2 dust → 2 items works for those too.

Mini-example: grind a diamond ore in the Macerator to get two diamond dusts, then run both through here. You end up with two diamonds from one ore — and only this machine can do that last step.

Energy

The Compressor only consumes EU/FE and keeps a small buffer so it doesn't stutter when the supply is uneven.

An LV machine — it draws up to 32 EU/FE per tick. Any surplus in the network isn't taken, the block isn't damaged. Energy comes in on the five working faces. The front face (the one the machine "looks" toward) is inert — cables don't connect there and hoppers don't work through it.

Processing

Each press takes 130 ticks (~6.5 s) and costs 260 EU/FE. The finished result doesn't eject on its own — pick it up by hand or with a hopper.

Recipes

The Compressor is crafted at a workbench (the same casing layout as the other LV machines). What it presses, grouped by job:

InputOutputWhy it matters
Non-metal dust (coal, diamond, emerald, lapis)the item backonly machine that restores these — closes non-metal ore-doubling
Metal dust (copper, iron, gold)ingotan alternate route to the Electric Furnace
Clay ballbrickpressing clay under pressure
Icepacked ice1:1, vs 9 ice at a crafting table
Packed iceblue ice1:1, vs 9 packed ice at a crafting table
Snow blockicea new route to ice, no loop back
Sugar canepaper1:1, vs the 3→3 crafting recipe

The headline: for coal, diamond, emerald and lapis, the Compressor is the only way to turn the dust back into the item — the Electric Furnace won't smelt them. That's what makes it the partner that completes ore-doubling for non-metals. (Lapis dust still gives ×1 back per dust, so there's no extra duplication: item → 1 dust → 1 item.)

Progression

Tier: LV. The densifying machine and the closer of the non-metal ore-doubling loop. It doesn't evolve into anything in v0.1. Needs power from an LV source, e.g. a Generator.

Related

Macerator — produces the dust this machine presses back into items. Electric Furnace — smelts metal dust (the Compressor is the non-metal counterpart). Electronic Circuit — a component along the processing chain. Generator — the power source.
