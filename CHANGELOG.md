## 0.1.37

<p><img alt="Zombies spawned wearing Ala Industrial hardened-iron armor" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AlaMine-Team-Org/AlaIndustrial/v0.1.37/release-media/v0.1.37/changelog.png" width="720"></p>

Zombies and skeletons can now spawn wearing the mod's hardened-iron gear, plus two fixes.

### New

- **Mobs can spawn in hardened-iron gear.** Like vanilla mobs that spawn in iron or copper armor, zombies and skeletons now rarely appear in the mod's hardened-iron armor — and a zombie may even carry a hardened-iron sword. Rarer on Easy, more common on Hard. Kill one and it may drop what it wore, so you can loot hardened iron instead of crafting it.

### Fixed

- **Machine hum comes back after teleporting.** After a `/tp` or dimension change, the hum of running machines went quiet until you rejoined. It now returns on its own at your new spot.
- **Fluid machines show their contents on hover.** Pumps, fluid tanks and geothermal generators now all show the liquid they hold in info-tooltip mods — the geothermal generator was the last one still showing empty.
