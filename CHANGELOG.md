## 0.1.18

<p><img alt="Battery Pouch — bundle-style storage with a charge bar" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AlaMine-Team-Org/AlaIndustrial/v0.1.18/release-media/v0.1.18/changelog.png" width="720"></p>

This update adds the **Battery Pouch** — the mod's first charged item. It's a carry-along storage bag that runs on EU: stash up to 128 stacks of items inside and keep it powered, or it locks itself shut until you recharge it.

### Gameplay

- **Battery Pouch** — a wearable storage bag that holds up to **128 stacks** of items (twice a vanilla bundle). It carries an internal **2000 EU** charge that slowly drains while it's in your inventory and has items inside. Let the charge run out and the pouch **locks itself** — you can't put anything in or take anything out until you recharge it. Insert items by right-clicking with them in your hand (it tops up existing stacks first), and right-click in the air to pull out the top stack.
- **Recharge it in the Battery Box.** A dedicated charge slot on the Battery Box fills the pouch at up to 32 EU/tick, so a full recharge takes just over 3 seconds. The slot only accepts the pouch (hoppers can't reach it).

### Quality of Life

- The **creative inventory** now shows Ala Industrial's items in the right vanilla tabs too — combat gear, tools and utilities, ingredients, building and natural blocks, and functional blocks all appear where you'd expect them. Tempered iron tools and armor are grouped together as iron-tier sets instead of being dumped at the bottom of the tab.

### Bug Fixes

- **Tempered iron tools can now be enchanted** at the enchanting table. Pickaxes, axes, hoes, shovels and swords made of tempered iron were previously missing from the vanilla tool tags, so the table offered no enchantments for them and the "Isn't It Iron Pick" advancement wouldn't trigger. They now accept their full vanilla enchantment set — Efficiency, Fortune, Silk Touch, Mending, Sharpness, Looting, and so on.
