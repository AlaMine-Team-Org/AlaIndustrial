## 0.1.31

<p><img alt="Ala Industrial 0.1.31 update preview: a player winding up a teleport jump" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AlaMine-Team-Org/AlaIndustrial/v0.1.31/release-media/v0.1.31/changelog.webp" width="720"></p>

Teleport home from the mine in five seconds, shut your machines up with a chip, and harvest crops without wrecking the field.

### New

- **Teleporter** — a station at your base, a remote in your pocket, and you're home. The station banks 500,000 EU: roughly 25–50 trips before a top-up. It pays for the jump, not the remote.
- **The jump is a scene** — five seconds of purple vortex and rising hum, the screen fades to black, and you're somewhere else. Move, take damage or die and it's cancelled — a cancelled jump costs nothing.
- **One remote, 16 stations** — name them or let them be Teleporter 1, 2, 3. Opens from either hand, and from a click on any plain block: a mine has no sky to point at.
- **A stolen remote is useless** — it binds to whoever first uses it. Stations are private by default, and both the privacy switch and the Delete button sit behind a padlock, so no stray click opens your base or erases home.
- **Upgrade panel on every machine** — 4 slots, tucked away until you open it. First chip: the **mute chip**, which shuts a machine up. Worth it once a row of macerators lives next door.
- **Metal gears** — stone, iron, gold, silver. Nothing uses them yet; groundwork for what's next.

### Quality of Life

- **Scythe: Shift to harvest** — one sweep takes only ripe crops, cactus and cane tops. Plain right-click no longer breaks crops at all, so clearing grass around a field is finally safe.
- **Electric furnace shows all 106 recipes it can smelt**, not just this mod's 17 — each priced the way the machine actually charges you.

### Changes

- **Scythe: 1 durability per block cut.** Grass used to be free to mow. It isn't anymore.

### Bug Fixes

- **Electric gear no longer drains in creative** — packs, pouches and the drill all stop spending charge there, like tools that don't wear out.
- **The energy pack charges the item on your cursor** and in the 2×2 crafting grid; it used to quietly stop.
- **Your gear talks to other mods' power** at 1:1 — their chargers fill our items, and can't siphon back out.
- **The drill's torches make a sound.** They were silent, which made a working feature feel broken.
- **Silver and Gold Chests appear in the creative tab.** They were craftable, just invisible.
