## 0.1.46

<p><img alt="Ala Industrial 0.1.46 fix patch" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AlaMine-Team-Org/AlaIndustrial/v0.1.46/release-media/v0.1.46/changelog.jpeg" width="720"></p>

A fix release: recipes finally show up in the Recipe Book, pipes and cables connect only where they actually work, and evolving a generator stops eating your spare chips.

### Fixed

- **Mod recipes appear in the Recipe Book.** 48 crafts and smelts — the tempered iron set, chests, pump, pipe, wrench, industrial workbench, ores and ingots — unlock as soon as you pick up their key ingredient.
- **The wind mill, its rotor and the electric furnace unlock properly.** Their entries asked for items the recipes never use, so they could stay hidden forever. Build with birch and never touch tin — the wind mill still shows up.
- **Evolving a generator no longer eats your spare chips.** The chip slot took a whole stack and destroyed all but one on the transform — up to 63 chips, which is 252 nickel ingots and 63 circuits. It now takes exactly one, and an older world's pile is handed back.
- **The item pipe connects to the iron furnace, solar panel, water mill and all three wind mills.** Feeding ore and coal in, or swapping worn parts out, works on every one of them now.
- **The water mill only takes a cable where power actually flows.** A cable used to snap to its sides, top and bottom and draw a joint that looked right and delivered nothing.
